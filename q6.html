6. WHAT DOES A FAIRER SYSTEM LOOK LIKE?


Fund social welfare and create more opportunities for black people.

The system is fair, you just have to work hard to succeed.



Job applicants with white-sounding names are 50% more likely to get a call back. Black people are shown 14% fewer homes and 4% fewer rental units than whites. Black drivers are 30% more likely to be pulled over. 67% of doctors have bias against black patients. This is not a level playing field.
