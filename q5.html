THE ANTI-RACIST POP QUIZ

5. IS IT POSSIBLE TO HAVE A WORLD WITHOUT COPS?


Crime will always happen and we need cops to uphold public safety.

We can decrease crime by solving for systemic racism.



Crime isn't random, it's a symptom of poor social infrastructure. In the words of former Police Chief David Brown: "We're asking cops to do too much in this country. Mental health, schools failing [...] that's too much to ask. Policing was never meant to solve those problems."
