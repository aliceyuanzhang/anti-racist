THE ANTI-RACIST POP QUIZ

5. IS IT POSSIBLE TO HAVE A WORLD WITHOUT COPS?


Crime will always happen and we need cops to uphold public safety.

We can decrease crime by solving for systemic racism.



Crime isn't random, it's a symptom of poor social infrastructure. Former Police Chief David Brown admits, "We're asking cops to do too much in this country. Mental health, schools failing ... policing was never meant to solve those problems."
