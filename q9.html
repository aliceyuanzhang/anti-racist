THE ANTI-RACIST POP QUIZ

9. WHAT ABOUT THE LOOTING?


Looting can cause serious damage to small businesses and low-income neighborhoods

Looting is a valid form of protest in a system that values property over human lives



Not all looters have the same affiliation and motives, but the act itself can be understood as an expression of anger and a bid for the redistribution of property. We should not focus on looting over the larger intention of the protests.
